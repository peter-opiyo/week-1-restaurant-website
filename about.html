Our Story

Rooted in Kenya. Crafted for modern dining.

Savanna Grill was born in 2019 from a simple idea: Kenyan food deserves a seat at the fine dining table. Founded by Chef Amani Odera, a Culinary Institute of America graduate who returned to Nairobi with a mission, the restaurant reimagines classic Kenyan dishes with modern techniques and presentation — without losing the soul of the food.

Our ingredients are sourced from farmers across Kenya. The tilapia comes from Lake Victoria. The vegetables are from organic farms in Limuru. The coffee is single-origin from Nyeri. We believe the best flavours come from the land, not from a supply chain.

What started as a 20-seat pop-up in Westlands has grown into one of Nairobi's most talked-about dining experiences. We now seat 80 guests across our ground-floor dining room and our open-air rooftop with views of the Ngong Hills.
